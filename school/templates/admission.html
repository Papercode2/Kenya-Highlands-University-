How to Get Admitted to Kenya Highlands University

Thank you for your interest in Kenya Highlands University. We welcome applications from qualified candidates.

Admission Process:

  1. Fill out the online admission application form available on our website.
  2. Pay the application fee online.
  3. Submit all required documents, including transcripts and recommendation letters.
  4. Wait for the admission committee's review of your application.
  5. Upon acceptance, you will receive an admission offer letter.
  6. Confirm your enrollment by paying the required fees.
  7. Welcome to Kenya Highlands University!
