Our Story

Kenya Highlands University is a leading institution of higher learning located in the heart of Kericho, Kenya. We have a rich history of providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and values to succeed in a rapidly changing world. We are committed to academic excellence, research, and community engagement.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of educators, researchers, and staff is here to support your educational journey. We are proud of our faculty's expertise and our vibrant student community.
